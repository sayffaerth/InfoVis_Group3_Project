Die durch die Coronafälle beschlossenen Maßnahmen
und das daraus resultierende Kaufverhalten in Deutschland.

Teilweise Einschränkungen
Strenge Einschränkungen

Prohibition to leave the apartment without sound reason.

Minimum distance of 1.5m to other people

Use of face masks on public transport or within shops is mandatory

Closure of non-essential shops, such as bookstores, warehouses

Closure of barbershops, hairdressers and related services in the field of body care (e.g., cosmetics studio, tattoo studio)

Closure of “essential shops" such as car and bicycle dealers, building and gardening supplies markets, pharmacies, drugstores, medical supply stores, petrol stations and banks (shops not relevant for nutrition and food)

Closure of zoos and botanic gardens

Prohibition to politically demonstrate in public and public gatherings

Closure of schools (including primary and secondary schools)

Closure of churches, mosques, or synagogues

Prohibition to stay in public space in the company of another person who does not belong to the same household

Prohibition to stay in public space in the company of several other persons who do not belong to the samehousehold

Prohibition to visit playgrounds

Closure of kindergarten or daycare
